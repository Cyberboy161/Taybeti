How to Use This Card

1

Go to the Homepage

Tap the button above to visit the homepage. From there, download and install Taybeti Notesharing on your Android device.

2

Open a Shared Note

Inside the app, tap the note that was shared with you. It may appear locked.

3

Enter the Passphrase

Type the passphrase exactly as written on this card. The note will decrypt and open.

4

Keep the Card Safe

Do not photograph this card. Do not share it. Keep your half in a secure place.

What is Taybeti Notesharing?

Taybeti means private and personal in Kurdish. This app is built for total privacy — every note is encrypted with its own key, nothing is stored in the cloud, and no one can read your messages except the person with the passphrase. Open source, free forever. Learn more →
