🔐 Taybeti

An open‑source, offline, encrypted note app for Android. Your notes stay on your device, protected by AES‑256‑GCM and Argon2id. No internet permission, no Google services, no backdoors.

Why Taybeti?

  • Zero‑trust master password (no hash stored)
  • Per‑note encryption keys
  • Covert sharing: encrypt messages as normal YouTube links
  • 100% offline – no ads, no tracking, no cloud
  • Open source (MIT license) – auditable by anyone
